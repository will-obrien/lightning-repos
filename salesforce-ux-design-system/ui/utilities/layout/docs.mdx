## Magnets

The magnet utilities are used to vertically attach adjacent card-like components.

The components/utilities that provide a card-like look are the following:

* [Cards](/components/cards/)
* [Page Headers](/components/page-headers)
* [Box](/utilities/box)

If you need a card-like component to appear flush to the card-like component below, you can add the classes  `slds-has-bottom-magnet` and `slds-has-top-magnet`.

<Example title="layout card">
<div className="slds-grid slds-grid_pull-padded slds-wrap">
  <div className="slds-col slds-size_1-of-1 slds-large-size_1-of-2 slds-p-horizontal_medium">
    <strong>Before</strong>
    <CodeView toggleCode={false}>
      <div>
        <Magnet />
        <Magnet />
      </div>
    </CodeView>
  </div>
  <div className="slds-col slds-size_1-of-1 slds-large-size_1-of-2 slds-p-horizontal_medium">
    <strong>After</strong>
    <CodeView toggleCode={false}>
      <div>
        <Magnet className="slds-has-bottom-magnet" />
        <Magnet className="slds-has-top-magnet" />
      </div>
    </CodeView>
  </div>
</div>
</Example>

In the after example, you'll notice the component's top and bottom side are flattened out and appear connected.
